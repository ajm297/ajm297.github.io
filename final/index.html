The Elements of Style

Spring 2013

This book was designed in an effort to make grammar more exciting for a younger reader. The cover was debosed by hand, and the book itself was assembled using perfect binding and hardcover accordingly.

When Life Gives You Lemons

Spring 2013

The goal for this project was to create a thoughtful way to display ones recipies. With the compact, string-closed box and modern color choices, this piece makes for a nice way to showcase your family recipes.

Avicii Poster Design

Fall 2012

This piece was done with the assignment being to portray a musical artist in a way that reflects their music style. Avicii's song, Levels, is an upbeat, base-heavy song, and I feel the poster portrayed that well with the use of the sound wave type chart.

Stamp Design

Spring 2013

While creating a cut-paper effect on this stamp series, the assignemt was to design a new series for the endangered species list. Atempting to show visually that these animals were endangered, they blend well with their surroundings, almost "disappearing" into the background.

Letter Form

Fall 2012

The goal of this assignment was to describe the word used within its own context. Hence, wet being my chosen word, is portrayed as looking wet and dripping.

Ten Things You Should Know

Fall 2013

I chose to write about ten things everyone should know about chinese takeout. Everybody orders it so going into depth about what you're consuming felt necessary. The content is pulled in from several sources including some of my own input, and the imagery is done using inDesign.
