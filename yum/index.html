Lemon Red Pepper Hummus Dip

  • 1(14oz.)can garbanzo beans, drained, liquid reserved
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons tahini (sesame seed paste)
  • ½ cup bottled roasted red bell peppers

Place

the garbanzo beans, tahini, lemon juice, garlic, salt and red peppers in the bowl of a food processor.

Add

2 tablespoons of the reserved juice from the garbanzo beans.

Pulse

until the mixture is smooth, scraping the sides and blade to mix completely.

Cover

and refrigerate until ready to use.
